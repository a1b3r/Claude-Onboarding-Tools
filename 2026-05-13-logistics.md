# Who Rides? Equity Gaps in Campus Bike-Share Access
*(Fictional sample article for practice purposes — Journal of Student Affairs, 2026)*

**Method.** Interviews (n=44) and a targeted survey (n=512) at two universities,
oversampling students living off-campus and students receiving financial aid.

**Findings.** Students on financial aid ride less despite reporting more interest:
barriers are the credit-card requirement at signup, fear of damage fees, and docks
concentrated near the most expensive housing. Where a campus removed the credit-card
requirement, the gap narrowed by half.

**Limitations.** Two campuses; interview sampling favored students already engaged
with campus services.

**Conclusion.** Low usage among some groups reflects access barriers, not preference —
program design changes can close most of the gap.
