# CLAUDE.md

Welcome to Recipe Box!!! This project was started in 2024 when our founder realized
that recipes were scattered everywhere. After months of brainstorming at a coffee shop
in Portland, the team decided that the world needed a better way to store recipes. We
believe deeply in the power of home cooking to bring people together, and our mission
is to make every kitchen a happier place. Our core values are Joy, Simplicity, and
Flavor.

The company grew from 2 people to 3 people in under a year, which tells you everything
about our trajectory. We hope to someday support every cuisine in the world.
