# Exercise 4 — Ethics Scenarios (groups of 3–4, 25 min)

**Goal:** practice the judgment calls before they happen for real. There are no
gotchas here — several scenarios have genuinely defensible answers on both sides,
and the discussion *is* the exercise.

## Format
Each group takes 2 cards (8 min each), then a full-room share of the spiciest
disagreement (9 min). Frame: "What would you do, and could you defend it in a
meeting?"

## Scenario cards

**1. The take-home assessment.** A job application includes a take-home data
analysis "to assess your skills." Nothing in the instructions mentions AI either
way. Do you use Claude? Fully? Partially? Do you disclose?

**2. The roommate's essay.** Your roommate asks you to "run my essay through your
Claude and make it better" the night before their deadline. Their course bans
AI use. It's their rule-breaking, not yours… right?

**3. The internship spreadsheet.** Your manager asks you to "use AI to analyze"
a spreadsheet containing customer names, emails, and purchase histories. The
company has no AI policy you know of. What do you do *before* you paste?

**4. The hallucinated citation, discovered late.** You find out a citation in a
report you already submitted (AI-assisted, unverified) doesn't exist. Nobody has
noticed. What now?

**5. The cover letter arms race.** Everyone's applications are AI-polished now.
Where's the line between "tailoring" and misrepresentation? Is there content in
*your* letter you couldn't speak to in an interview?

**6. The biased shortlist.** You used AI to screen 80 resumes down to 10 for a club
leadership role, and someone points out the shortlist skews heavily one direction.
What's your responsibility, and what would you change about the process?

## Debrief anchors (facilitator)
- Almost every scenario resolves to the same three moves: **know the written policy,
  disclose when in doubt, and never submit what you can't defend.**
- For #3 specifically, land the rule: personal data needs authorization *before*
  pasting — see guide [Module 09](../../guide/index.html#m9).
